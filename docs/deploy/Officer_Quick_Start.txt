Dodgeville PD Scheduler — Officer Quick Start
=============================================

LOG IN
------
  Username: officer   Password: officer
  Link your account to your roster name via Admin if needed.

YOUR TABS
---------
  Command Post     — My Week schedule and alerts
  Leave & Swaps    — submit vacation, training, court, or leave requests
  My Timecard      — enter hours for the current pay period
  Duty Timeline    — visual schedule for the rotation window

REQUEST TIME OFF
----------------
1. Leave & Swaps → pick date and type → Submit.
2. Track status in My Day Off Requests (pending, approved, denied).
3. You receive a notification when a supervisor acts on your request.

TIME CARD
---------
  Hours count in the pay period where the shift *starts* (overnight too).
  Use Import from Schedule to pre-fill from your live rotation.
  Submit for supervisor approval when the period is complete.

VIEWING SCHEDULE
----------------
  Base Schedule  — rotation template
  Live Schedule  — includes approved bumps and swaps
  Duty Timeline  — Gantt-style week view

NOTIFICATIONS
-------------
  Command Post → Alerts, or the badge on Command Post in the sidebar.

NEED HELP?
----------
  Ask a supervisor to refresh data (sidebar → Refresh All).
  Profile: click your name in the top bar for pay-period and hours summary.
