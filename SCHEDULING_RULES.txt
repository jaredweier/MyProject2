REQUEST WORKFLOW
----------------
  Officer submits  -->  Pending
         |
  Supervisor Approve
         |
    +----+----+
    |         |
  Bump OK?   No --> Pending Manual Review --> Supervisor Approve --> Approved
    |              (supervisor override; skips auto bump check)
   Yes
    |
  Approved (override + replacement if found)

  Supervisor Reject (from Pending or Manual Review) --> Rejected (final)

STATUS SUMMARY
--------------
  Pending                  - Awaiting decision; Approve or Reject
  Pending Manual Review    - Auto bump failed; Approve (override) or Reject
  Approved                 - Final; override on file
  Rejected                 - Final

- Re-approve / re-reject of finished requests: BLOCKED
- Approve is one transaction: if override fails, request stays Pending
